Midwood High School 1997 Yearbook

Sep 8, 2017

In 2017, I set out to find a copy of the yearbook for the 1997 graduating class of Midwood High School in Brooklyn, NY. It proved to be impossible to find one online. Luckily, the Midwood Alumni Association had one copy in their archives, and they were kind enough to allow me to photocopy the pages.

I'm making the photos of the yearbook available here. The following are posted with permission from the Midwood Alumni Association.
