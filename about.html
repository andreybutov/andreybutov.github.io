About me

The Basics

My name is Andrey Butov. I'm a founder and developer at Antair, a small software shop where I design and build mobile, desktop, and web applications.

I currently live in New York, with my wife, our two kids, and our dog Charlie.

The best way to reach me is by email at andreybutov@antair.com. You can also find me on Twitter at @andrey_butov.

Educational Background

I hold a Masters degree in Computer Science from Columbia University.

Work Background

My early work focused on building large-scale trading systems for various Wall Street firms.

Since founding Antair in 2005, I have built and shipped numerous software products, for a wide variety of platforms.

These days most of my work is centered around building custom iOS and Android applications for select clients.
